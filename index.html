Sound Bomber

Ever wanted to send sounds to friends? Get tired of boring "making noises" the old fashioned way with your mouth? WELL WORRY NO MORE!

INTRODUCING... SOUND BOMBER!

Sound Bomber allows users to connect to Facebook and send push notifications to their friends that make noise when received by the other user.

BUT WAIT, THERES MORE:

Sound Bomber can also be used locally to make noises, featuring fun animations and the ability to put any picture on the speaker. THAT'S RIGHT, ANY PICTURE can be blended into the speaker for INFINITE amounts of fun.

BUT THAT'S NOT ALL:

Sound Bomber also features iWatch support, allowing users to instantly respond to their friends' sounds with sweet, sweet revenge from their wrists. Users can also use their watches to send farts to their phone locally.
